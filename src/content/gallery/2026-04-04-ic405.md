# PART 2: THE CONTENT (The "Story")

## Capture Notes
This was my first attempt at a multi-panel mosaic. I spent three nights gathering the data. The transparency was excellent on the second night, though the wind picked up during the Ha sessions.

## Processing
Processed entirely in **PixInsight**. 
1. WeightedBatchPreprocessing for calibration.
2. GraXpert for gradient removal.
3. Spectrophotometric Color Calibration (SPCC).
4. BlurXTerminator followed by StarletTransform for stretching.

The Ha data was added to the Red channel using the NBRGBCombination script to make the star-forming regions pop.
