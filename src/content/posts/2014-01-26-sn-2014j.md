Along with other members of [Bedford Astro Society](http://bedsastro.org.uk), got some nice view of the recent SN in [M82](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Messier_82 "M82 - The Cigar Galaxy").
I still need to resolve some guiding issues with the RC8 - would be nice to get some images of this before it fades!
An excellent image from Adam Block can be seen on the [APOD](http://apod.nasa.gov/apod/ap140124.html) site...
 
Also had some nice view of Jupiter, including a Ganymede shadow transit, the Eskimo Nebula, M81 and of course, Orion!
