I took the opportunity of some scarce free time at the weekend (1st Feb '14) and rooted and flashed my Asus Prime. Scary stuff for a novice at this sort of thing, but it went very smoothly!
 
Big kudos to the guys on [this forum](http://forums.androidcentral.com/asus-transformer-pad-infinity-700/336920-tutorial-flashing-custom-rom-tf700t.html "Android Central") and 'berndlb' in particular for the tut - made it really easy to follow along and do!
 
Result? Well, I have a sexy [CROMi-X](http://forum.xda-developers.com/showthread.php?t=2223492 "CROMi-X") custom ROM with all the Asus bloatware stripped out, and the tab runs along very nicely now, it is usable once again!
 
I'm now having fun looking at apps that need root to run, and exploring what the device can really do!
