Tuesday 18th March 2014. Save the date. This is when I become famous.....
 
Haahaahaa!! Well, if you can call a one minute interview becoming famous... The [Sky At Night](http://bbc.co.uk/skyatnight) team paid a visit to the [Bedford Astronomical Society](http://bedsastro.org.uk) on the 18th March to film a piece about Mars for the April program.
I was lucky enough to be recorded with Pete Lawrence about imaging Mars - and he was very complementary despite Mars behaving like a small red bouncing ball!!
It was very interesting to watch the filming take place and see how they put the sequences together and they were a great of people to work with!
You can see some images I took of the evening in my [Photo Gallery](https://darrenjehan.me.uk/home/assets/gallery/index.php?album=skyatnight)  and there are sets on Flickr from the members of [BAS](http://www.flickr.com/photos/bedsastro/sets/72157642609613365/).
