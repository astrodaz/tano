The sell-off of the Atik 383L+ and related kit goes well.... Just the OAG and the camera itself now, and I have interested parties in both, so fingers crossed.
 
The new camera is the [Atik One](http://www.firstlightoptics.com/atik-cameras/atik-one-60-mono-camera.html "Atik One") which has an integrated 5 position filter wheel, off-axis guider and the intro offer comes with a planetary/guide camera as well!
This will get me back into lunar imaging as well as being the guide camera.
I saw both at this year's AstroFest and was impressed by the specs and build quality. Looking forward to early March when they get released!
